Process

01

Planning

This initial stage involves understanding the project's goals, target audience, and content requirements. It includes defining the website's purpose, functionality, and overall structure, often through wireframes and sitemaps.

02

Design

In this phase, the visual aesthetics and user experience (UX) are crafted. This includes creating mockups, choosing color schemes, typography, and imagery, and refining the user interface (UI) to ensure an intuitive and engaging experience.

03

Development

This stage brings the design to life through coding. It involves writing the actual HTML for structure, CSS for styling, and JavaScript for interactivity, transforming static designs into a functional and dynamic website.

04

Testing & Launch

The final stage focuses on ensuring the website is fully functional, bug-free, and performs optimally across various devices and browsers. After thorough testing and quality assurance, the website is deployed to a live server, making it accessible to the public.
